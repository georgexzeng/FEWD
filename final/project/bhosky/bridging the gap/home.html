We are everything but a literary magazine. Briding the Gap is an online forum for those who want their voices heard, felt, analyzed. It is a democratic medium for the open exchange of all forms of literature and the commentary it inspires. You must create an account to showcase and receive community feedback on your work, but do not need to create an account to critique and rate others’ work.

Voices in Space is the perfect place for you and your fellow writers to share ideas discuss each other’s work. Even if you don’t have a formal workshop yet, you can just start a group invite your friends!
